# Code of conduct

Be respectful, specific, and evidence-led. Debate claims and sources without attacking contributors. Do not upload private voice recordings, biometric data, or cloned samples without documented permission.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, doxxing, and deceptive impersonation are not accepted. Report conduct or privacy concerns privately through the repository security contact. Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove material that puts a person at risk.
